Guidance

Signpost links allow you to create direct links to content you know people are looking for.

They can be used with or without an image.

Grouped together they can be used to build a simple list of links or sub navigation. This may be useful if you have several links around a particular area or theme.

Accessibility

This component passes the [Co-op accessibility framework].

[Co-op accessibility standards]

Content design

The link text should be concise and actionable. Tell the user where the link is taking them.

[Co-op accessibility standards]

Line length should be kept short. This means people can find what they want quickly and the size of the card/button does not become excessive.
