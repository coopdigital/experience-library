Accessibility guidelines

Make it work for everyone.

At Co-op we want anyone to be able to use our products and services. We have committed to this in our accessibility policy

This means we need to design, build and set things up in a way that is considerate of a range of needs. The guidance, tools and resources here will help you to do this.
