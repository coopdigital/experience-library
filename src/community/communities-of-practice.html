Typical sessions include talks, design crits or exploring a particular topic together.

All colleagues are welcome to join any session.

Whoever is currently running the community of practice will post the topic for each session in the relevant channels in Slack:

  • #content-designers
  • #interaction
  • #servicedesigning
  • #userresearch
  • #visual

To get a calendar invite for any community of practice, ask one of the facilitators.
