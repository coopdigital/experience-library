Add to the Experience Library

If you want to add something to the Experience Library, or you think current guidelines need to be completely changed, add your suggestion to the Component Lounge Miro board.

Suggest an update to the Experience Library

If you want to suggest an update to existing guidelines – like making something clearer, changing the examples or correcting inaccuracies – fill in our feedback form.

How contributing to the Experience Library works

At the moment, only Co-op colleagues can suggest an addition or update to the Experience Library.

You can check whether it’s already on our to-do list

Our to-do list is the first column of our design delivery Trello board.

If it is on our to-do list

Add any additional evidence, research or examples to the Trello card.

If it’s not on our to-do list

If it’s a new addition to the Experience Library, add your suggestion to the Component Lounge Miro board.

If you’re suggesting an update, fill in our feedback form.

If you want to work on an addition or update

If you want to be involved in adding or updating something in the Experience Library, add:

  • yourself to the relevant ticket in the design delivery Trello board
  • your name, on a post-it, to the relevant component card in the Component Lounge Miro board

What happens next

When we’re ready to review your suggestion, we’ll:

  1. Arrange an informal chat, along with with anyone who's said they’d be interested in working on the suggestion.
  2. If agreed, prioritise it in our to-do list and involve you as much or as little as you want.
  3. Because of the amount of things already on our to-do list at the moment, our initial chat may take a while to arrange.

Thank you

The Experience Library exists because of the experience, knowledge and skills of lots of people from around the Co-op. It would not exist without your contribution.

Your contribution will help:

  • our colleagues and communities work more efficiently
  • our products and services be more inclusive
  • save Co-op money
