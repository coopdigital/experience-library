Guidelines

The quote should help your user to:

  • understand the content better
  • do what they came to do
  • trust the information

You could use quotes from:

  • an expert
  • someone who has attended an event
  • someone who has used a product or service

Reference who you got the quote from. This can include the person's:

  • name
  • job title
  • organisation
  • website, if relevant

We usually avoid using italics, but referencing the source of a quote is an exception.

If you use a quote within a quote, use double quotation marks around it.

Examples

“Having accessible services should not be a question for Co-op, it’s what we should and must do.”

Adam Warburton, Chief Product Officer, Co-op.
