Guidelines

Usually quotes come from an expert or someone who has used a product or service.

If you’re using a quote on a digital platform, the quote should help your user to:

  • understand the content better
  • do what they came to do

Examples

‘Use hanging punctuation to style a quotation. A “quote within a quote” should use double quotation marks.’

The design team

‘Or you can have a bigger one.’

The design team

Quotations used with the standard or larger font size applied.
