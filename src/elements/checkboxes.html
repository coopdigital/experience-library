Guidelines

When using checkboxes you should:

  • make clear which checkboxes are selected and which are not
  • allow people to click or tap anywhere in the checkbox or label to select that option

You should also display the options a person can choose from top to bottom, not left to right. This means that:

  • people can scan the options
  • people who have zoomed in to the content will not have to scroll side to side to work out which checkbox they need to select

Let people tell you that the available options do not apply to them. For example, by providing a ‘None of these’ option. Some people need this to be a clearly stated option, so that they can move on to the next question or task.

All attributes of the checkboxes are designed using values from the foundations. Do not change the styling of the checkboxes or the associated styles.
