Our foundations are the design basics that we build our products on.

We use foundations to help create our elements and components.

When we keep our foundations consistent, our products look and feel consistent for customers.

Design tokens

In engineering, foundations are sometimes called design tokens.

Design tokens are our foundations represented as data.

Design tokens let us maintain and update data around the foundations in one place.

  • Borders

  • Colours

  • Drop shadow

  • Font

  • Font sizes

  • Radius

  • Spacing
