Who it's for

This training is for people who already have a basic understanding of digital accessibility.

What you'll learn

Learn more about how to create products, services and communications that everyone can understand.

The training will:

  • help you build empathy and awareness of digital accessibility
  • give you confidence when talking about accessibility
  • make it easier to encourage your team to prioritise accessibility

When the sessions are

The 1.5-hour sessions take place monthly, but we can also arrange a session to fit around your team.
