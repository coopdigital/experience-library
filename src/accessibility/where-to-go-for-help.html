Co-op's accessibility champions

We're a multidisciplinary team across design, product, delivery and engineering that formed out of a passion for making a difference and including everyone.

Our aim is to make Co-op's products and services accessible to all.

Antonia Duffin, Design Operations Officer

Training and support

If you'd like accessibility advice or a second opinion on a product or service, the accessibility champions hold bi-weekly drop in sessions on Mondays at 3pm.

Reminders are sent out via Slack channels, or alternatively you can contact Antonia Duffin on Microsoft Teams to book your slot.

If you want to learn more about digital accessibility and would like to attend our Digital Accessibility Awareness training, contact Antonia Duffin to book your place.
