Who it's for

This training is for people who have no knowledge of digital accessibility.

What you'll learn

Try out our impairment simulation kit to experience some of the difficulties people with vision and mobility impairments face.

Learn about:

  • digital accessibility and why it's important
  • the social and medical models of disability
  • why it's important to not rely on simulation
  • how to think about accessibility in your work or communications

When the sessions are

We can fit around you and your team.
